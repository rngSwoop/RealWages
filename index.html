RealWages

Where your wages meet reality

Saunder VanWoerden, Carter Jacobs, Colton Laird, Garrett King

What is RealWages?

RealWages is a website that allows you to see how inflation affects your pay.

How does it work?

Enter your current pay and the year you started working. We will calculate how much you would be making today.

Why does it matter?

It's important to know how inflation affects your pay. If you don't know how inflation affects your pay, you could be losing money!
